Handibus Concept

Positive awareness of difference

A unique human and sensory experience thanks to the "handi bus", a pedagogic bus concept to raise awareness about disability in a fun way. To take the place of a disabled person for an innovative journey, to understand his daily life ... this is the promise of the Handi Bus. Incorporating the most modern technologies, the concept allows an experiential approach to disability by reversing roles.

For what purposes ?

Acquiring another perspective on disability

Make easier the contact with the disabled person

Know how to adopt the right attitude

Facilitating the integration into the world of work

Who is it for ?

  • Companies
  • Schools
  • Communities
  • Associations
  • Social centers

L'aménagement

Hearing impairment

Introduction to hearing disability using a digital workshop to discover a few words of the sign language.

Physical disability

Space dedicated to the technical difficulties encountered on a daily basis by wheelchair users.

Visual impairment

Set up of play games and simulation via the virtual mask

Awareness spaces

The interiors and exterior of the bus are totally modular, which allows to propose customized workshops according to the public and the objectives of the operation.
